ABOUT ME:

Hello! I’m Onesti Brookins, a Media Arts & Science student based in Indianapolis, IN. My journey in Game & Web Design/Development began with a passion for computers, video games, and how they can be used to teach, help, and entertain people, and it has since evolved into a fulfilling journey. When I’m not coding and designing, you might find me playing video games, drawing, and making music. These experiences inspire me and keep my creativity flowing, helping me bring fresh perspectives to my work. Feel free to explore my portfolio to see some of my recent projects. If you’d like to connect or learn more about what I do, don’t hesitate to reach out via my contact page or send me a message. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to connecting with you!
